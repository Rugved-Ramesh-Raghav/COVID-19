Wash Your Hands Regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for 30
seconds will help you avoid germs.

coughing in public. Even better, use a mask.

Stay Home Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or being in crowded places. Prefer
staying at home.

Eat Healthy

Avoid consuming junk food.Eat as healthy as possible
to boost your energy.

Stay Calm

Regular excersize or meditation is a great way to keep
yourself calm and composed.
